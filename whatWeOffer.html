Our specials

1.Chicken

  • Our Chicken farmer is Paul Martin, from Wallenstein
    ON(St.Jacobs area).
  • Small Mennonite farm, family owned & operated.
  • Free run birds,raised with twice the allotted space to roam the barn.
  • 100% antibiotic free, through their whole life cycle.
  • Birds are fed a custom,non -medicated feed with flax and no animal bu-products.
  • Chicken are processed on the farm with no cages and long distance travel,reducing stress to the birds.
  • Chicken are processed on Wednesday,using the water chilled sysytem,delivered to us on on Thursday.
  • Upon arrival to the shop,whole chicken are broken down into pieces for sale.
  • Also available, liver and other organs, chicken feet and bones for soup or broth (chickenbox).

2.BEEF

  • Our Beef farmer is Ed,from Cambridge ON.
  • 100% Grass-fed and Grass finished.
  • Famer grown chemical free diet of grasses, clover and legumes.
  • Traditionally raised in a closed cow-calf herd. All cattle are born & raised on the farm.
  • Abbattoir shares neighbouring land,limiting stress to the animals for processing.
  • Beef is dry aged for 21 days before being delivered to our shop for sale.
  • Available to purchase: Premium steaks,various butcher cuts and roasts,ribs,sausages,ground beef.
  • By popular demand we sell the organs of the animal and bones for soup broth.

pork

  • Our piggery farmers are Fred & Ingrit Martines, from Perth Pork, located in the Stratford area.
  • Family-owned farm,specializing in heritage breeds.
  • Raised in the barn for shelter with access to open fields to root & graze.
  • Antibiotic free.
  • Pigs are processed on the farm to reduce stress to the animal.
  • Tamworth, Berkshire,Duroc pigs all carried in a variety of cuts. Bacon,smoked ham,chops,roasts, ground meat and sausages with no added fillers.

Turkey

  • Raised by the Snyder family in Breslau, ON (near Waterloo).
  • Farmer raised GMO free feed with added vitamins, minerals and probiotics.
  • 100% antibiotic free.
  • Turkeys are fresh frozen at the time of processing and arrive to us as a frozen product.
  • Available for sale are whole turkeys for the holidays, individual bird pieces, ground turkey, sausage with no fillers.
